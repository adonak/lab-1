Annual Precipitation Across the United States, 2015 to 2021

This map shows the total annual precipitation for each year between 2015 to 2021 for a number of cities across the United States.

To use it, you can zoom by using the scroll wheel on a mouse or by clicking the plus (+) and minus (-) icons in the upper left-hand side of the map. Additionally, you can pan by clicking and dragging the cursor over the map.

To cycle through different years, click on the forward or backward arrows or drag the button along the line in the controller on the lower left-hand side of the map.

Lastly, you can search the map for a city or other location in the search bar in the upper right-hand side of map.

Data from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA)
